Standard triple tank

Reinhardt

PRO

1. required pick(if enemy has a rein)
2. fast charging ult
3. high melee damage

CON

1. kited easily
2. needs constant healing
Zarya

PRO

1. Decent damage at all ranges
2. can save teammates
3. grav engages

CON

1. can be energy starved at times
Roadhog

PRO

1. hook
2. 300health self-heal
3. high damage at close/midrange

CON

1. poor damage outside of midrange
2. bullet sponge
Soldier 76

PRO

1. highly moblie w/
rocket jump + sprint
2. aoe heal
3. strong damage at long ranges
4. can quickly burst down most heroes
5. can kite tanks well

CON

Ana

PRO

1. shutsdown enemy healers
2. heals incredibly fast
3. strong cc

CON

1. targeted by flankers
Lucio

PRO

1. speedboost for peal or pushing
2. aoe heal
3. highly moblie

CON

1. weak damage
2. slow charging ult

INFO

Ana's heals is what makes this comp work. She can keep those three tanks alive with ease assuming she has LOS(line of sight). All the other tanks should play around the reinhardt and ana. It can be pretty hard to kill 3 grouped up tanks.

MAPS

This comp can be played on all maps on both defense and attack.

SWITCHES

You can switch out the solider for pretty much any dps. Roadhog can be switched out for d.va as well. On defense mercy or zen replacing lucio is fine as well.

INFO

Call out a single player on the enemy team and everyone focuses that one target, Winston jumps, Lucio speed boosts, Genji dashes, Tracer blinks, Zarya bubbles winston, while Ana trys to keep everyone alive.

CON

PRO

1. low cool down escape
2. high risk/high reward ult

CON

1. weak damage
2. slow without teammates

INFO

Set up ethier on the payload, or on high ground, and KEEP BASTION ALIVE. Use defense matrix, Zarya bubble and rein shield near the Bastion to block damage. Healers want to pocket Bastion whenever possible.

MAPS

Strong on most attack and defense maps, weak on koth.

SWITCHES

On attack lucio or zen replacing mercy is fine but normally mercy is better at pocketing.
